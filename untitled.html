Abstract

Introduction

Beer type are often identified with their taste, where dark beers have strong tastes and IPA/APA have distinct bitterness. Is it possible to link these respective taste to fluorescence components or with absorption coefficient?
Here, we sampled beers from Quebec's micro- and macrobreweries to determine which niche each of them occupy; how do they contribute to the beer offer in Quebec and to evaluate de capacity of brewerer to adequately identifies their beers type.
